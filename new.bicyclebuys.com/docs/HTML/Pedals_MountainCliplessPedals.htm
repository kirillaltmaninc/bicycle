BikeHard City Pro Clipless Pedals
The BikeHard City Pro pedal is a product of some of the most logical developments in pedal technology. Conveniently designed to work with both standard footwear and cycling specific shoes, one side is a platform and the other has an SPD compatible clipless system. An alloy body and alloy cage is built on a CNC cromoly axle and rolls on premium sealed cartridge bearings. The platform side has 4 specially designed adjustable anti-slip contact points, which are easily raised or lowered with a 4mm allen wrench. The clipless side has a micro-adjustable SPD binding tensioned by high end square springs. Since these pedals use the same cleats as spin bikes, they are a pedal of choice for those who want to upgrade their bicycles for clipless outdoor riding. Weighing in at a super light 352 grams per pair, these pedals are suitable for all bicycles, including hybrids, comfort bikes, road bikes, mountain bikes, cruisers.
