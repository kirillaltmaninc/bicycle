The Park Tool BBT-39 Bottom Bracket Bearing Tool Set is designed for installation and removal of the bearings used in these new bottom bracket systems. The BBT-39 includes two bushing sets, which are used in conjunction with a Park Tool Bearing Cup Press (HHP-2 or HHP-3), to precisely press the BB30 and BB90 bearings into the bottom bracket shell.
